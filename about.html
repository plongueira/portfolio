About

I studied Computer Engineering in A Coruña (Spain), where I developed a strong foundation in algorithms, systems and computational thinking.

My approach to software engineering is strongly architecture-driven. I prefer understanding systems as a whole rather than isolated components, with a focus on scalability, correctness and performance.

I currently work as an external consultant in the banking sector in Spain, contributing to backend systems that operate in production environments with strict reliability and performance requirements.

Interests: distributed systems, backend architecture, performance engineering, numerical systems.
